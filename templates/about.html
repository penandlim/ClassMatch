About

In 2013, Jared Zoneraich and Paras Modi created ClassMatch to make the process of finding classmates for next year more efficient and easier! ClassMatch is a website that enables you to quickly and easily find your classmates for the upcoming year!


In 2014, John Lim forked the project and modified to fit Ramapo High School's AB schedule style. It was renamed to 'RamapoClassMatch' to avoid confusion with original ClassMatch.
